Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our handwriting recognition technology

How accurate is your handwriting recognition?

Our technology achieves over 95% accuracy for most handwriting styles. The accuracy may vary depending on the clarity and style of handwriting.

What file formats do you support?

We support common image formats including JPG, PNG, and others involved with image files containing scanned handwritten text.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we take data security seriously. All uploads are encrypted and we don't store your images or extracted text longer than necessary for processing.

Do you offer an API for developers?

Yes, we provide a RESTful API for developers who want to integrate our handwriting recognition technology into their applications. If you want it, contact us!
